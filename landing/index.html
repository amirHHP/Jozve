Never lose an idea or note again. Capture it quickly, right where you work.

DropIt is a Chrome extension designed to capture notes, research, and fleeting ideas without interrupting the natural flow of browsing.

Key features of DropIt

Quick Capture

Instantly capture any selected text, ideas, or annotations from your browser without leaving your active tab.

Bilingual

Complete dual-language Farsi (RTL) and English (LTR) workspace design supporting your preferred layout.

Dark / Light

Beautiful visual styling modes optimized for daylight note taking and night-time research sessions.

Offline-first

Your notes are stored locally and securely in Chrome storage. No remote servers, 100% private and accessible offline.

Why I built DropIt

I wanted a place to keep my notes organized by source and category—something that feels more like a digital notebook built around the way I actually read and browse. I also wanted it to be lightweight, offline, and always within reach—something that helps learning instead of interrupting it.

How to use

  1. 1
    Download or clone the repository
  2. 2
    Open Chrome Extensions (chrome://extensions)
  3. 3
    Enable Developer Mode in the top right
  4. 4
    Click "Load unpacked" button
  5. 5
    Select the DropIt extension directory
  6. 6
    Start capturing your knowledge effortlessly!
